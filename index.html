We have collected all the databases that are available online. None of the above databases have the information regarding human response to tuberculosis infection. Drug resistant tuberculosis is still unsolved. Poor patient's compliance, lack of detection of resistant strains and unavailable therapy are key reasons for the development of drug resistant tuberculosis. Each year the disease kills almost two million people.
